Privacy Policy

Last updated · 15 May 2026

Smaran ("we", "our", "the app") is built with privacy as a first principle. This policy explains what we do — and don't — collect.

TL;DR

Smaran does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data to any server. Everything you create in Smaran lives only on your device. There are no advertisements. There is no tracking. There are no third-party analytics in this version of the app.

What we store on your device only

When you use Smaran, the following information is saved to secure storage on your device:

  • Your name (the one you entered during onboarding)
  • Your chosen ishta-devata (the deity you feel closest to)
  • Your streak count and last-open date
  • The list of verses you have saved as favorites
  • Your notification preference

This information never leaves your device. We do not have a server. We cannot see this information. If you uninstall Smaran, this information is permanently deleted.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any account identifier
  • Your location
  • Your device ID or advertising ID
  • Your contacts, photos, calendar, or any other on-device data
  • Your behavior in the app (which buttons you tap, when you open the app, which verses you save)
  • Any analytics, telemetry, or crash reports beyond what Google Play automatically provides to app developers (anonymous crash counts)

Notifications

Smaran sends you one local notification per day at 7:00 a.m. local time, naming the day's deity. This notification is generated entirely on your device — no server is involved. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings → Apps → Smaran → Notifications.

Sharing

When you tap the share button on a verse, Smaran opens your device's native share sheet. You choose where to send the verse (WhatsApp, SMS, email, etc.). The shared message is composed on your device. We do not receive a copy.

Children's privacy

Smaran is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Future updates

As Smaran grows, we will add features that may involve:

  • Cloud accounts (so your streak survives across devices)
  • AI-generated wisdom responses ("Ask Krishna")
  • Premium subscriptions via Razorpay
  • Audio downloads
  • Festival content packs

When any of these are added, this policy will be updated to clearly explain what new data is collected, how it is used, and how you can control it. You will be notified within the app of significant changes.

We commit to: never selling your data, never running third-party advertising on Smaran, and always letting you export or delete everything we hold about you.

Third-party services we use today

The current MVP uses only:

  • Expo / React Native — the app framework. No data transmitted.
  • Google Play Services — required for Android distribution. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

When future versions add Supabase, Razorpay, RevenueCat, or AI providers, those will be listed here with links to their privacy policies.

Your rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to request, correct, or delete on our side. Everything is on your device — you control it directly through your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
