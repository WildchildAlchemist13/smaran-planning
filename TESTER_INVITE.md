## Instructions for testers (paste below the link)

If anyone asks "how do I install":

```
1. Tap the link on your Android phone
2. You'll see a Google Play page that says "Smaran" with "Become a tester"
3. Tap "Become a tester" — this adds you to the internal list
4. Wait 30 seconds, then tap "Open in Google Play"
5. You'll see "Install" — tap it
6. Open Smaran when it's installed
7. Walk through the 4-step welcome (name, your favorite deity, allow morning notification)
8. You'll see today's deity and verse — that's the daily home
9. Open it tomorrow morning at 7 a.m. when the bell rings

If anything weird happens, screenshot it and send it to me.
```

## What to ask testers (after 5-7 days)

Don't survey them at install. Wait a week. Then 3 questions over WhatsApp:

```
Hey [name], how's Smaran been treating you?

3 quick questions:

1. Have you been opening it daily? (Yes / kind of / no — all answers fine)
2. What's the one thing that bugged you most?
3. What's the one thing you'd want next?

If it's been a beautiful little ritual, I'd love to hear that too. Either way, your honesty helps me build it right. 🙏
```

---

## How to handle feedback you receive

| Tester says | What to do |
|---|---|
| "It's beautiful, I love opening it" | Save the message — this is your launch testimonial later |
| "I keep forgetting it's there" | Diagnose: did notification fire? Does their phone block it? Is 7am wrong for them? |
| "Boring without the AI / art / audio" | Acknowledge — those are coming. Don't rush to ship them. |
| "It crashed when I…" | Get screenshot + Android version + steps to reproduce. Add to RISKS.md and fix in next build. |
| "What's [Sanskrit word]?" | If multiple testers ask, the onboarding needs to explain better. Note it. |
| "My mom would love this" | "Send her the link! Same opt-in works." |
